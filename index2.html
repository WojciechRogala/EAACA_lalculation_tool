Calculation Methodology

The EAACA Calculator is designed as a professional engineering tool for assessing the global warming potential (GWP) of wall partitions.

Norms and Standards

Calculations are performed in accordance with European standards EN 15804+A2 (Sustainability of construction works) and EN 15978 (Assessment of environmental performance of buildings).

Life Cycle Assessment (LCA)

The analysis covers the full 50-year life cycle of the partition (stages A-D): from raw material production (A1-A3), through construction, to the use phase (B) - including recarbonation and insulation replacement - ending with demolition (C and D).

Environmental data sources

The algorithm uses third-party verified Type III (EPD) provided by manufacturers (e.g., Xella Polska) and recognized generic databases like ÖKOBAUDAT.
